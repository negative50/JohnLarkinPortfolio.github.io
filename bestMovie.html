My Favorite Movie and Actor

This is a tough decision between "American Ultra" and "Scott Pilgrim Vs. The World". I would just call it a tie and say both are my favorite.

American Ultra

Scott Pilgrim Vs. the World

I like "Scott Pilgrim vs. the World" a lot because I respect the director, Edgar Wright,who made that movie because he also made "Hot Fuzz" and "Shaun of the Dead". I also have been reading the visual novel series which the movie was created from and I just thought that this movie portrayed the visual novel well and did so in a way that felt original and creative.

I like "American Ultra" not because it was a good movie, because it wasn't really, but I just liked the concept of the movie and the aesthetic of it. I think, if I had to choose a favorite actor, which I can't but I'll pick someone anyways, then it would have to be Robin Williams.

Robin Williams is a hugely iconic actor that has been part of many people's childhoods from movies like "Jumanji", "Robots", "Good Morning, Vietnam", and "Aladdin". He's like the uncle we've always had.
